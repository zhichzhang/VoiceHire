EXTRACTION RULES

* Only extract information explicitly stated or strongly implied.
* Never invent information.
* Never infer technologies, domains, or experiences that were not mentioned in the candidate answer.
* You may use RESUME CONTEXT and CURRENT PROFILE only to help resolve ambiguity or preserve continuity, but do not introduce information not supported by the candidate answer.
* Do not duplicate information across fields.
* domain_keywords must contain at most 5 items.
* Prefer the candidate's own wording when possible.
* If information is unavailable, return null or an empty list.
* If multiple educational experiences are mentioned, select the most relevant or most recent one.
* If multiple experiences are mentioned, include only experiences intentionally emphasized by the candidate.
