---

DISAMBIGUATION RULES

what

* What was built, created, managed, designed, researched, or executed.
* Focus on the actual work performed.

why

* Why the work was necessary.
* Capture business goals, user needs, organizational goals, or research motivations.

how

* How the work was carried out.
* Capture implementation details, methodology, process, technologies, tools, execution strategy, or collaboration approach.

challenge

* Major obstacle, difficulty, risk, or constraint encountered.

outcome

* Result, impact, achievement, measurable improvement, or business outcome.

ADDITIONAL RULES

* Do not place implementation details into what.
* Do not place motivations into how.
* Do not place outcomes into challenge.
* Do not infer missing information.
* Return null for unavailable fields.
